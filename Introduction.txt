GAURAV'S GUESS GAME

The motive of this game is to read mind of the PC.
Cow is used for match of the digit in the number but not at exact place.
Bull is used if the digit matches at exact places.
If you want to give up, give your guess as 0.

RULES:
1. Enter the 3 digited number as your guess.
2. The numer should be a non zero number.
3. The digits of the number should not repeat.

If at any moment you feel like quiting the game just enter 0 as your guess.
